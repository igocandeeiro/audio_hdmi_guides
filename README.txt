Requirements
  A. OS X
	1. El Capitan/10.11 - Initial support
	2. Yosemite/10.10 or newer
	3. Mavericks/10.9 or newer
	4. Mountain Lion/10.8 or newer
  B. AppleHDA.kext
	1. Native
	2. Patched

Notes
  A. Audio ID Definitions
	1 - HD4600+/AMD/Nvidia HDMI audio with 3/5/6 audio port analog audio
	2 - HD4600+/AMD/Nvidia HDMI audio with 3 audio port analog 5.1 surround sound
	3 - HD3000/HD4000 HDMI audio with analog audio (Line Out/Orange - NA)
